overview



We are the Digital Medication Management team of University of Waterloo's School of Pharmacy.Our team includes experts from the areas of pharmacy, knowledge translation and information sciences. Our mission is to work with the medication management to promote safe and effective use of medication through digital solutions such as: infographics, informational videos, flashcards and more.





tasks



Graphic Designer

Designed and created informative materials for students, pharmacists, doctors, health management as well as for the public to promote safe and effective use of medication. I was responsible for condensing large information into summarized points for educational modules, such as flashcards and infographics, as well as create vector graphics to match the given information.
